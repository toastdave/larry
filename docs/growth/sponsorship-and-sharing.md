# Sponsorship And Sharing Guardrails

## Share surfaces that are live now

- Persona booth invite links from chat
- Seeded prompt sharing that preserves the selected persona without exposing private transcripts
- Booth recap cards that package the opening take, latest read, and source context without copying the whole thread

## Acceptable sponsor surfaces

- Landing-page modules
- Clearly labeled recap cards or side modules outside the main transcript
- Optional account-level upgrade or partner modules that do not affect ranking or answer content

## Surfaces that stay off-limits

- Inline ads inside assistant replies
- Sponsored ranking boosts in citations or search results
- Interruptions inside the composer or streaming transcript

## Disclosure rules

- Every sponsor placement must be labeled as sponsored or partner content
- Sponsor creative cannot mimic assistant messages or citation cards
- Sponsor content must sit in its own visual container away from the answer stream

## Ranking separation rules

- Sponsor relationships never change retrieval ranking
- Sponsor relationships never change citation choice
- Sponsor analytics can observe engagement with labeled modules, but not rewrite the answer path

## Early metrics

- Share-link copy rate
- Return visits that start from a shared booth link
- Upgrade clicks that happen after a shared session starts
- Sponsor module click-through rate, only after disclosure and ranking-separation rules are in place
