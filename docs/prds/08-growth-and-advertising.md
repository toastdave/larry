# Growth And Advertising

## Goal

Explore growth loops and sponsorship models that support the business without poisoning the trust or rhythm of the product.

## MVP scope

- Referral, sharing, and social hooks planning
- Sponsored placement rules for surfaces outside the core chat stream
- Ad eligibility guardrails and disclosure requirements

## Requirements

- Ads or sponsor placements must never alter factual ranking or citation choice.
- Sponsored content must be visibly labeled.
- The main chat composer and response stream should remain interruption-free.
- Growth features should amplify the product voice rather than dilute it.

## Task breakdown

- Define acceptable sponsor surfaces such as landing pages, recap cards, and side modules.
- Document disclosure and ranking separation rules.
- Explore referral incentives that align with paid conversion.
- Define metrics for sponsor performance without degrading retention.

## Acceptance criteria

- Sponsored placements are clearly separated from chat answers.
- The product team has written guardrails before ad experiments launch.
- Growth tactics preserve trust and do not make Larry feel cheap.

## Non-goals

- Inline ad injections inside assistant replies
- Auto-playing media units in chat
